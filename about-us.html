CELENA RESTAURANTS

Founded in 1997, Celena Restaurants comprises the restaurant business of acclaimed chef, restaurateur, TV personality and author Celena.

It is one of the largest privately-owned restaurant groups in the UK, with additional restaurants internationally across the USA, France, Dubai, Singapore and more.

As well as fine dining, the group has expanded to include more casual dining restaurants offering a wide variety of guest experiences around the globe such as Bread Street Kitchen & Bar, Celena Bar & Grill, Celena Steak and Celena Hell’s Kitchen.
